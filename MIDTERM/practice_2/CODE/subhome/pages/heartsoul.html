Your Heart VS. The Home Button

THE FACTS:
The home button is the iPhone’s ripcord. If you get lost, you can just push the button below and be airlifted back to safety.

RELATION TO DAEMON:
Home is where the heart is! Have you ever wondered why the base button on your iPhone is referred to as the “home”? Consider this- maybe technology is intended to forge or fill some sort of void we have in the physical realm. Just as your heart is thee central point of your body, the home button is the same of your phone. What does that say about our perceptions of “home”?
