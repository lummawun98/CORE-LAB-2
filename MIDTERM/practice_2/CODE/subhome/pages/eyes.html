Your Eyes VS. The Screen

THE FACTS:
High exposure to high levels of HEV light can damage tissue in the retina of the eye. This can lead to permanent vision loss in adults.

RELATION TO DAEMON:
We have become so obsessed with what is going on in the virtual worlds that we create for ourselves on social media and such that we have stopped paying attention to reality and lost a part of the connectivity that comes along with real life human interactions.
