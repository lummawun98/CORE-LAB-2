Your Brain VS. The Speaker

THE FACTS:
Research shows that noise levels above 10 decibels strip insulation from the nerve fibers carrying signals from the ear to the brain. This eventually can disrupt electrical nerve signals in your brain resulting in multiple sclerosis.

RELATION TO DAEMON:
Although speakers can be used for things such as listening to music or tool for communicating with others, if we are not mindful of our usage with them, we could be giving these small devices more power than we realize to harm us in significant ways.
